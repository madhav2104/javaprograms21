SECTION 4 — SUMMARY


The redesign of the customer and system metadata collection model addresses critical challenges of data duplication, inconsistent schemas, and governance risks across the enterprise data platform. By introducing a centralized Canonical Data Model with Customer Master and System Metadata entities, the solution eliminates redundant storage of key attributes, enforces a single, unified customer identifier, and ensures that PII is handled in compliance with governance policies. Transactional and event data reference these master entities via foreign keys, supporting efficient joins and enabling a unified view for business analytics. The architecture is designed for scalability, compatibility with platforms such as Databricks, and robust governance, positioning the organization to reduce costs, improve data quality, and empower business teams with reliable, actionable insights. This initiative lays a strong foundation for future data-driven innovation and operational excellence.
